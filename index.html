About Us

Premium Ready Mixed Concrete

Ideal Conmix Ready Mixed Concrete plant in Bangalore has been responsible for redefining the pace and quality of construction activity in metropolitan areas for mega infrastructure projects.

Small building projects and individual home builders seeking high levels of quality assurance can also now usher in the same sophistication and value addition into their construction as some of the mega projects using ready mixed concrete- at virtually no extra cost.

Ready Mixed Concrete is manufactured under computer-controlled operations and transported and placed at site using sophisticated equipment and methods. Ready Mixed Concrete assures its customers numerous benefits.

Our Services

Ready Mix Concrete

Our Ready Mixed Concrete is custom-made to suit different applications. Manufactured at modern fully computerized plants with state-of-the-art machinery and equipment by suppliers of global repute.

Concrete Pumping

We operate mobile and stationary pumps capable of discharging concrete at high elevations and over long distances, ensuring efficient and timely placement at your work site.

Concrete Mix Design

Our marketing team includes qualified and experienced engineers and concrete technologists who can assist customers in defining and specifying quality standards and designing specific concrete mixes.

Hollow Bricks

Our Hollow Bricks are manufactured with precision to ensure strength, durability, and eco-friendliness. Designed for load-bearing and non-load-bearing structures, they offer excellent thermal insulation, reduce construction costs, and contribute to faster building times. Ideal for both commercial and residential projects.
